About us

Welcome to Dunder Mifflin


Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, Inc., is a paper supply company with its corporate office in New York; it has six branches in eastern states of the USA. The CEO of Dunder Mifflin is David Wallace. Each Dunder Mifflin branch is run by a branch manager who supervises all employees at the branch. All branch managers are supervised by the Dunder Mifflin CEO. The CEO is not supervised by anyone.
